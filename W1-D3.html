Focaccia with caramelized onions, cranberries, and brie

Wow Your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorie holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the foaccia:

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinger
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thougn it's not necessary)
  • i 1/2 cups fresh or frozen eranberries

Method

  1. Make The focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actualli knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
